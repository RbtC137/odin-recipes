Korean Banana Milk

Description:

A cup of ice cool banana milk is really good for summertime. You can make one yourself by following this recipe within just few steps.

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 4 to 5 ice cubes(optional)

Steps:

  1. Combine banana, milk, water, maple syrup and vanilla in the blender cup. Blend until smooth.
  2. Place ice cubes in galss(if using). Pour banana milk on top.
